CPR/AED Training

Learn life-saving techniques with our comprehensive CPR and AED training program.

Our CPR/AED training follows the latest HSI curriculum aligned with American Heart Association (AHA) guidelines and teaches local teams how to recognize cardiac emergencies and properly perform CPR for adults, children, and infants. You'll also learn how to use an Automated External Defibrillator (AED) with confidence for real workplace and community scenarios.

What You'll Learn:
  • How to recognize the signs of cardiac arrest
  • Proper CPR techniques for all age groups
  • AED operation and maintenance
  • Relief of choking
  • How to work as part of a resuscitation team

Duration: 2-3 hours

Certification: Valid for 2 years

First Aid Training

Comprehensive first aid training for emergency situations.

Our First Aid training equips local participants with the knowledge and skills to provide essential care in workplace and community emergencies. From treating minor cuts to managing serious injuries, this hands-on course prepares your team to respond confidently and effectively.

What You'll Learn:
  • Assessment of emergency situations
  • Treatment for bleeding and shock
  • Management of burns and fractures
  • Response to sudden illnesses
  • Recognition and treatment of heat/cold emergencies

Duration: 4-6 hours

Certification: Valid for 2 years
