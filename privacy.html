Privacy Policy

Crisis Ready Solutions LLC

Effective Date: May 20, 2024

Introduction

At Crisis Ready Solutions LLC, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you visit our website and our practices for collecting, using, and protecting that information.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us through our contact and scheduling forms, including:

  • Name, email address, and phone number.
  • Organization or company name.
  • Training interests and preferred dates.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and schedule training sessions.
  • Provide the services you requested.
  • Process certifications through HSI or AHA.
  • Send you administrative information or updates regarding your training.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with certification bodies (like HSI) solely for the purpose of issuing your training credentials.

Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to request access to, correct, or delete any personal information that you have provided to us.
