AHA-Aligned Training Philosophy

Crisis Ready Solutions is committed to teaching CPR and First Aid techniques that follow the latest American Heart Association (AHA) guidelines and standards.

As an HSI-certified training provider, we ensure that every course we deliver is aligned with AHA's evidence-based protocols, which are updated every five years based on the latest scientific research. This means you're learning the most effective, proven techniques used by medical professionals and emergency response teams worldwide.

What This Means for You:
  • Evidence-Based Learning: Techniques proven to improve survival rates from cardiac arrest
  • Global Recognition: Your certification is honored worldwide and meets OSHA requirements
  • Professional Standards: Learn the same protocols used by paramedics, nurses, and emergency responders
  • Continuous Updates: We stay current with the latest AHA guidelines to provide you cutting-edge training
